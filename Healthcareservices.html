About Our Healthcare Initiatives

DSR & DJR Foundation is dedicated to improving access to quality healthcare for underprivileged communities. We focus on preventive care, early diagnosis, and affordable treatment to ensure healthier lives.

Our Vision

To build a society where every individual has access to essential healthcare services regardless of financial status.

Our Mission

  • Provide free and affordable medical services
  • Promote preventive healthcare awareness
  • Support critical treatments for the needy
  • Improve maternal and child health
