About Our Social Welfare Programs

DSR & DJR Foundation works towards social equality and community development by supporting vulnerable and marginalized groups. Our welfare programs aim to improve quality of life and promote dignity for all.

Our Vision

To create inclusive communities where every individual has access to basic needs, opportunities, and social justice.

Our Mission

  • Support disadvantaged communities and families
  • Promote social equality and human dignity
  • Provide relief during emergencies and disasters
  • Encourage community participation and development
