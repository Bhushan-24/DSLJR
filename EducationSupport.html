About Us

DSR & DJR Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to promoting education and equal learning opportunities for underprivileged and deserving students. We believe education is the most powerful tool to break the cycle of poverty and build a brighter future for individuals and communities.

Founded with a vision of social responsibility and inclusive growth, the foundation focuses on providing financial aid, academic resources, and mentorship to students across various educational levels.

Our Vision

To create a society where every child and youth has access to quality education, regardless of their economic or social background.

Our Mission

  • Support students from economically weaker sections through educational assistance
  • Encourage academic excellence and skill development
  • Reduce school and college dropout rates
  • Promote lifelong learning and self-reliance
